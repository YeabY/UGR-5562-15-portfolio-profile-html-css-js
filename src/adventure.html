Adventures

Hawassa

Hawassa, a charming city by Lake Hawassa, was a peaceful and refreshing getaway. I spent my days enjoying the serene lake views, with its calm waters reflecting the colorful skies during sunrise and sunset. A highlight was taking a boat ride where I encountered hippos and vibrant bird species like pelicans and kingfishers. The local fish market was a fascinating experience, offering a glimpse into the daily life of the city while sampling freshly prepared fish dishes.

Entoto Natural Park

Entoto Natural Park offered an unforgettable combination of serene nature and thrilling activities. The highlight of my visit was the exhilarating zipline ride, soaring over the lush forests and meandering streams. The breathtaking views from above made the adrenaline rush even more memorable. Beyond the zipline, the park's forested mountains, dramatic waterfalls, and hidden rock formations provided a perfect backdrop for hiking and exploration. It's a place where adventure meets tranquility, leaving you in awe of Ethiopia's natural wonders.

Kulubi

My visit to Kulubi was a deeply spiritual experience. I traveled there to celebrate a biannual Orthodox Christianity spiritual ceremony, which takes place on Tahisas 19 and Hamle 19 every year. The atmosphere was filled with vibrant traditional dances, hymns, and the devotion of the community. The celebration was a profound cultural experience that deepened my connection to Ethiopia's religious heritage. The spiritual energy of the place and the warm hospitality of the people made this adventure unforgettable.

Debrezeit

Debrezeit, with its picturesque lakes and lush greenery, provided a delightful escape. One of the highlights of my trip was visiting the Kuriftu Water Park, which offered a fun and refreshing experience. The water park is set against the beautiful backdrop of Lake Bishoftu, providing a perfect blend of relaxation and excitement. I enjoyed swimming, water slides, and other recreational activities, all while soaking in the stunning natural surroundings. Boat rides on the lake were equally relaxing, and the area's peaceful vibe left me rejuvenated, making it one of my favorite spots to visit in Ethiopia.

Bahirdar

Bahirdar, known for its scenic beauty and cultural richness, was a destination I visited for a family wedding. Experiencing traditional Ethiopian wedding customs amidst the vibrant celebrations was truly memorable. I also explored the breathtaking Lake Tana and the majestic Blue Nile Falls, both of which showcased the region's incredible landscapes and added to my unforgettable journey.
